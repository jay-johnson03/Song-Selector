Song Selector

Why use this site?

This site is designed to help you choose a song based on your mood or activity. Whether you're looking for something upbeat to energize your workout, a mellow tune to relax after a long day, or a specific genre to match your current vibe, our song selector has got you covered.

  • Reason 5 : Improves the move

Here is some additional information!

This website will be set up as a store front to help you find the best songs for your mood. There will not be any actual purchases, but you will be able to click on links to go to Spotify to listen to the songs.

To use this website, click on the drop menu to select a mood of activity. Then scroll through the list of songs that match your selection. There will also be a few playlists to choose from

Once you find a song or playlist of your choosing, you click on the "go to song" or "go to playlist" button and it'll directly link you to Spotify to listen to the song or playlist.

Enjoy your music!
